Tiwani

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

This policy should be reviewed by the organisation owner or legal adviser before final App Store submission.

Overview

Tiwani is a private association management app for members, administrators, treasurers, and electoral officers. The app helps the association manage membership records, events, dues, library documents, announcements, marketplace listings, polls, and elections.

Information We Collect

  • Account information such as name, email, phone, role, status, and profile photo.
  • Profile and family information such as address, marital status, birthday, spouse details, anniversary, and children details when provided.
  • Finance information such as dues, charges, payment records, outstanding balances, and audit references.
  • Event information such as RSVPs, attendance, and check-in records.
  • Voting information such as poll participation, election voter receipts, and aggregated vote results.
  • Marketplace information such as listing details, image URL, status, and seller contact information.
  • Library document metadata and uploaded files.
  • Notification preferences, read state, and push tokens.
  • Technical information such as crash reports, app version, device platform, and diagnostic logs.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide app access, maintain association records, manage events and dues, deliver announcements and reminders, operate polls and elections, support marketplace enquiries, protect the app, and keep audit logs for privileged actions.

Sharing

Tiwani does not sell member data. Information may be shared only with authorised association officers, Firebase and Google Cloud services, app store platform services, and legal or regulatory parties where required.

Member Directory Privacy

Members and electoral officers may view a sanitized member directory. The directory must not expose private finance fields, payment references, notification preferences, or administrative metadata.

Voting Privacy

For secret-ballot elections, Tiwani keeps voter receipts separate from ballot selections. Administrators may confirm that a member voted, but should not see which candidate that member selected.

Retention And Deletion

Records are kept as long as needed for association operations, reconciliation, governance, legal compliance, audit trails, and dispute resolution. Members can request account deletion in the app. Some records may be archived or anonymised rather than deleted.

Security

Tiwani uses Firebase Authentication, Firestore rules, Storage rules, Cloud Functions, role checks, organisation checks, and audit logs to protect data.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the support contact published in the app or on the public support page.
