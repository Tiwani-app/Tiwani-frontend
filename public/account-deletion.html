Account Deletion

Last updated: June 18, 2026

How To Request Deletion

Members can request account deletion inside the app:

  1. Open Settings.
  2. Open Account Deletion.
  3. Enter the reason for the request.
  4. Submit the request for administrator review.

If the member cannot access the app, they should contact the published support contact and include the email address associated with the account.

What Happens Next

An administrator reviews the request. If approved, Tiwani may delete or disable the Firebase Authentication account, anonymise the member profile, remove push notification tokens, and preserve records required for finance, governance, election integrity, or audit purposes.

Records That May Be Retained

  • Finance reconciliation records.
  • Legal, tax, or governance records.
  • Election voter receipt verification records.
  • Audit logs and abuse prevention records.
  • Records needed for dispute resolution.

Expected Timing

The association should review account deletion requests promptly. Completion may depend on identity verification, outstanding obligations, and administrator availability.

Contact

For account deletion questions, use the public support page.
