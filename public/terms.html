Tiwani is intended for authorised association members and approved administrators. Access may be suspended or removed if a user is no longer eligible, violates association rules, or misuses the app.

Account Responsibility

Users are responsible for keeping their login credentials secure and notifying the association if they suspect unauthorised access.

Acceptable Use

  • Do not attempt to bypass app security, rules, or role restrictions.

Tiwani records may be used by authorised officers for membership, finance, event, governance, voting, and audit purposes. Finance and governance records may be retained even after an account is closed.

Polls and elections must be conducted according to association rules. Secret-ballot elections should keep voter participation records separate from ballot choices.

The app may occasionally be unavailable due to maintenance, network issues, Firebase service issues, or device/platform problems. The association may update these terms as the app, policies, or legal requirements change.
