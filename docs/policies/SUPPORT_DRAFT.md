# Tiwani Support Page Draft

Last updated: June 18, 2026

This draft is for production review before publication. It should be hosted as the public Support URL.

## Getting Help

For app support, contact the association administrator or the published support contact.

Include:

- Your full name.
- The email address used for Tiwani.
- Your device type.
- A short description of the issue.
- A screenshot if helpful.

Do not include passwords, private payment references, or sensitive personal data in support messages.

## Common Support Topics

- Login and password reset.
- Membership profile updates.
- Event RSVP or check-in issues.
- Dues, charges, and payment records.
- Polls and elections.
- Marketplace enquiries.
- Library document access.
- Notification preferences.
- Account deletion requests.

## Emergency Or Sensitive Issues

For suspected account compromise, incorrect finance records, or voting concerns, contact an administrator directly as soon as possible.
