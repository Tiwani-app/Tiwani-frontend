# Tiwani Account Deletion Policy Draft

Last updated: June 18, 2026

This draft is for production review before publication. It should be reviewed before being used as the public Account Deletion URL.

## How To Request Deletion

Members can request account deletion inside the app:

1. Open Settings.
2. Open Account Deletion.
3. Enter the reason for the request.
4. Submit the request for administrator review.

If the member cannot access the app, they should contact the published support contact and include the email address associated with the account.

## What Happens Next

An administrator reviews the request. If approved, Tiwani may:

- Delete or disable the Firebase Authentication account.
- Anonymise the member profile.
- Remove push notification device tokens.
- Preserve necessary finance, governance, election receipt, and audit records.

## Records That May Be Retained

The association may retain records needed for:

- Finance reconciliation.
- Legal, tax, or governance obligations.
- Election integrity and voter receipt verification.
- Audit logs and abuse prevention.
- Dispute resolution.

## Expected Timing

The association should review account deletion requests promptly. Actual completion may depend on identity verification, outstanding finance/governance obligations, and administrator availability.

## Contact

For account deletion questions, use the public support contact published in the app.
