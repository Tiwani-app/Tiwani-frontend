# Tiwani Terms Of Use Draft

Last updated: June 18, 2026

This draft is for production review before publication. It should be reviewed by the organisation owner or legal adviser before being used as the public Terms URL.

## Eligibility

Tiwani is intended for authorised association members and approved administrators. Access may be suspended or removed if a user is no longer eligible, violates association rules, or misuses the app.

## Account Responsibility

Users are responsible for keeping their login credentials secure and for notifying the association if they suspect unauthorised access.

## Acceptable Use

Users must not:

- Attempt to access another member's private data.
- Submit false membership, finance, event, marketplace, or voting information.
- Misuse push notifications or announcements.
- Upload harmful, unlawful, or inappropriate documents.
- Use the marketplace for prohibited or unsafe goods.
- Attempt to bypass app security, rules, or role restrictions.

## Association Records

Tiwani records may be used by authorised officers for membership, finance, event, governance, voting, and audit purposes. Finance and governance records may be retained even after an account is closed where retention is necessary.

## Voting

Polls and elections must be conducted according to association rules. Secret-ballot elections should keep voter participation records separate from ballot choices.

## Marketplace

Marketplace listings are member-to-member unless clearly stated otherwise. Tiwani and the association are not automatically the seller, buyer, guarantor, delivery provider, or payment processor for member listings.

## Availability

The app may occasionally be unavailable due to maintenance, network issues, Firebase service issues, or device/platform problems.

## Changes

The association may update these terms as the app, policies, or legal requirements change. Continued use after updates means the user accepts the updated terms.

## Contact

For support, contact the association support contact published in the app or on the public support page.
