# Tiwani Marketplace Rules Draft

Last updated: June 18, 2026

This draft is for production review before publication. It should be reviewed before being used as the public Marketplace Rules URL.

## Purpose

The Tiwani marketplace lets association members list items for other members to enquire about directly.

## Listing Rules

Listings should be accurate and include:

- Clear title.
- Honest description.
- Fair condition label.
- Price.
- Valid contact route through the listing owner or administrator.

## Prohibited Listings

Members must not list:

- Illegal goods or services.
- Weapons or dangerous items.
- Counterfeit items.
- Stolen goods.
- Medical, controlled, or regulated items without proper authority.
- Offensive, discriminatory, or unsafe content.
- Anything that violates association rules or applicable law.

## Enquiries

The Enquire button may open WhatsApp, SMS, email, or another configured contact method with a pre-written message. Members should verify all details before paying or arranging delivery.

## Responsibility

Marketplace transactions are between the buyer and seller unless the association clearly states otherwise. Tiwani does not automatically guarantee item quality, payment, delivery, refunds, or disputes.

## Moderation

Administrators may edit, hide, archive, or remove listings that violate these rules or association expectations.

## Safety

Members should avoid sharing sensitive information unnecessarily and should arrange exchanges safely.
