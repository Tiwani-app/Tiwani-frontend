# Tiwani Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: June 18, 2026

This draft is for production review before publication. It should be reviewed by the organisation owner or legal adviser before being used as the public Privacy Policy URL.

## Overview

Tiwani is a private association management app for members, administrators, treasurers, and electoral officers. The app helps the association manage membership records, events, dues, library documents, announcements, marketplace listings, polls, and elections.

## Information We Collect

Tiwani may collect:

- Account information: name, email address, phone number, role, membership status, and profile photo.
- Profile and family information: address, marital status, date of birth, spouse details, wedding anniversary, and children details when provided by the member.
- Finance information: dues, charges, payment records, outstanding balances, and audit references.
- Event information: RSVPs, attendance, and check-in records.
- Voting information: poll participation, election voter receipts, and aggregated vote results. Secret-ballot elections keep voter participation separate from selected choices.
- Marketplace information: listing title, description, price, image URL, status, and seller contact information.
- Library information: document metadata and uploaded files.
- Notification information: announcement read state, notification preferences, and device push tokens.
- Technical information: crash reports, app version, device platform, and diagnostic logs needed to operate and secure the app.

## How We Use Information

Tiwani uses information to:

- Provide member access to the app.
- Maintain accurate association records.
- Manage events, check-ins, dues, payments, and member standing.
- Deliver announcements and event reminders.
- Operate polls and elections.
- Show member directory information while hiding private finance fields from ordinary members.
- Support marketplace enquiries between members.
- Protect the app from abuse, errors, and unauthorised access.
- Keep audit logs for privileged actions and governance review.

## Sharing

Tiwani does not sell member data. Information may be shared only as needed with:

- Authorised association administrators and officers.
- Firebase and Google Cloud services used to host authentication, database, storage, functions, push notifications, and crash reporting.
- App stores and platform services as required for app delivery and operation.
- Legal or regulatory parties where required by law or association obligations.

## Member Directory Privacy

Members and electoral officers may view a sanitized member directory. The directory may include contact and family profile information, but it must not expose private finance fields, payment references, notification preferences, or administrative metadata.

## Voting Privacy

For secret-ballot elections, Tiwani keeps voter receipts separate from ballot selections. Administrators may confirm that a member voted, but should not see which candidate that member selected.

## Data Retention

Tiwani keeps records as long as needed for association operations, finance reconciliation, governance, legal compliance, audit trails, and dispute resolution. Some records may be archived or anonymised rather than deleted.

## Account Deletion

Members can request account deletion from the app. Tiwani may anonymise profile data while retaining necessary finance, governance, election receipt, and audit records.

## Security

Tiwani uses Firebase Authentication, Firestore security rules, Firebase Storage rules, Cloud Functions, role checks, organisation checks, and audit logs to protect data. No system can guarantee perfect security, but the app is designed to limit access to authorised users and roles.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the association support contact published in the app or on the public support page.
