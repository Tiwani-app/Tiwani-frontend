# Tiwani Member User Guide

Last updated: June 23, 2026

This guide is for members and electoral chairmen using the production app.

## Sign In

Use the email address approved by the association. If you cannot sign in:

- Confirm you are using the correct email.
- Use password reset.
- Contact an admin if Firebase Authentication succeeds but the app says your
  profile cannot be restored.

Authentication and member profile approval are separate. A Firebase Auth account
must also have an active Tiwani profile.

## Dashboard

The dashboard shows your available actions based on role:

- Events
- Vote
- Members
- My Ledger
- Marketplace
- Library
- Notifications/settings

Admins see additional management actions.

## Profile And Family Details

Members can edit safe profile details such as:

- phone
- address where allowed
- date of birth
- marital status
- spouse details
- wedding anniversary
- children/dependants
- notification preferences

Members cannot change their own role, account status, finance status, or
balance.

## Member Directory

Members and electoral chairmen can view other member profiles for approved
directory information and family context.

Hidden from normal members:

- other members' finance records
- balances
- payment references
- private admin-only metadata

## Events

Members can:

- View current and past events.
- RSVP to available future events.
- See elapsed events when selecting their date.
- Receive event reminder notifications when push is enabled.

## Voting

Members can vote in eligible polls/elections.

Secret ballot instructions:

- Choose carefully and review your selection before submitting.
- You can submit once.
- The system records that you voted.
- For secret ballots, admins/electoral chairmen should not see your selected
  candidates.
- Expired polls/elections may remain visible for reference/results.

## Finance

My Ledger shows your own dues, charges, payments, and balance.

If a payment or charge looks wrong:

- Use the finance contact buttons where available.
- Contact the treasurer for payment support.
- Contact the dues creator for questions about a specific charge.
- Include the charge title/reference in your message.

## Library

The library contains association documents such as:

- constitutional documents
- meeting minutes
- reports

Only published member-visible documents appear to members.

## Marketplace

Members can view listings and enquire directly through the configured contact
method. The enquiry message should include the listing title and product
context.

## Notifications

Notifications and read/unread state are per user. Reading a notification on one
account should not mark it read for another account.

Push notifications require device permission.

## Account Deletion

Members can request account deletion from Settings. Deletion is reviewed and
completed through the backend/admin process.

## Getting Help

When reporting an issue, include:

- your role
- screen name
- approximate time
- what you expected
- what happened
- screenshot if safe

Do not send passwords, private payment references, or other members' private
data.
